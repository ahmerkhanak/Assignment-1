BEEF BIRYANI DESCRIPTION



Beef Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic Pakistani dish made with tender beef, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of spices. The beef is slow-cooked in a rich and savory gravy, then layered with fluffy rice and steamed to perfection. This hearty and comforting meal is a staple of Pakistani cuisine, perfect for special occasions or everyday enjoyment.
