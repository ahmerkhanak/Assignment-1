COLA NEXT DESCRIPTION



Cola Next is a Pakistani beverage brand that offers a similar taste and experience to Coca-Cola. With a similar color scheme and packaging design, Cola Next aims to provide a comparable cola taste at a potentially more affordable price point. By choosing Cola Next, consumers in Pakistan can support a local brand that aligns with their values and contributes to the growth of the Pakistani economy. Additionally, Cola Next offers a viable alternative for those who wish to boycott Israeli products, promoting a sense of solidarity and economic empowerment in the region.
